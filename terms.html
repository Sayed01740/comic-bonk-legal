04 Auto-Reconnect & Local Data Storage

To provide a seamless experience, Comic Bonk stores your wallet's public address on your device after you connect for the first time. This allows the app to:

  • Automatically reconnect your wallet session when you reopen the app
  • Let you start the game without reconnecting your wallet each time
What is stored locally: Only your public wallet address, unlock status, and game high scores. No private keys, seed phrases, or sensitive information is ever stored.

You can clear all stored data at any time by using the "Disconnect" option in the game. Uninstalling the app will also clear all locally stored data.
