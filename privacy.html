Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 24, 2026

Comic Bonk respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Wallet Connection

Comic Bonk connects to Solana wallets via Seed Vault secure signing. We never store private keys or seed phrases.

Guest Mode

Users may play in Guest Mode without connecting a wallet.

NFT Minting

NFT minting is optional. Users approve transactions themselves and may pay Solana network fees.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal data directly. Basic technical data such as IP address or device info may be logged by hosting or blockchain services.
