01 Information We Process

1.1 Guest Mode

If you choose to use the "Play as Guest" feature:

  • No wallet connection is required
  • No blockchain interaction occurs
  • No personal data is processed or stored
  • No cookies or usage IP is recorded for sessions

1.2 Wallet Information (Connected Access)

When you connect a Solana wallet to Comic Bonk, we may process:

  • Your public wallet address
  • Authorization messages or identity verification (if applicable)
  • On-chain transaction history
  • NFT mint details, events, or access status
We do NOT collect, store, or access:
✕ Your private keys
✕ Your seed phrases or recovery phrases
✕ Your wallet passwords

Wallet connection uses Solana Seed Vault / Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter signing only. Your keys never leave your secure device environment.

1.3 Data Stored on Your Device

To provide a seamless experience, Comic Bonk may store limited data locally on your device:

  • Game State / Progress — Your app automatically caches game state. If you clear app data, this data will not be recoverable.
  • High Scores — Your personal best scores are stored locally on device.
  • Local Wallet Session — Connection preferences are stored through your wallet provider for auto-reconnect functionality.

1.4 Blockchain Data

Blockchain transactions conducted through this app are publicly recorded on the Solana network. This data is public and immutable by default — we do not control or modify blockchain data visibility.

02 Technical Data

To maintain performance and stability, we may process limited technical data:

  • Crash logs and error reports
  • Device type and OS version
  • General usage analytics strictly to improve stability and app performance

Our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) and RPC providers may collect standard IP addresses and device information for security and content delivery purposes.

03 How We Use Information

  • Direct wallet interactions and auto-discovery on the blockchain
  • Display user wallet balance or username/identity, if provided
  • Facilitate NFT "Gaming Pass" minting (optional, may require SOL network fees)

04 What We Do NOT Do

Our commitment to your privacy:
✕ We do NOT collect private keys or seed phrases
✕ We do NOT sell your data
✕ We do NOT use behavioral tracking

05 Third-Party Services

Comic Bonk may rely on the following third-party infrastructure. These services process data under their own privacy policies:

  • Solana Network — For blockchain state, transactions, and RPC endpoints
  • Arweave Gateways — For accessing decentralized metadata for NFTs
  • Supabase — For backend services and hosting
  • GitHub Pages — For hosting web resources and legal pages

06 Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures, procedures, and technical safeguards. We do not store sensitive data such as passwords on our servers. Data collected for provisioning purposes is removed when no longer necessary.

07 Children's Privacy

Comic Bonk is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

08 Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. This will not diminish our commitment to transparency. Material changes will be communicated on the app's launch screen where appropriate.
